About

A poem, by Nick: "Barry Wally is really fast. Like so fast. So fast he is like a flash of light. Like the flash of light. The flash."

All jokes aside, the Flash makes a really good use of speed. He is the fastest man alive, even faster than Superman. He taps into the speed force and with the help of his friends, fights crime such as Zoom, Reverse Flash, and along side the Justice League. His predcessor is Jay Garrick (Crimson comet) and Wally West (Kid Flash).
